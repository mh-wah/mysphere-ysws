# MySphere - a hack club ysws 

*You might even learn some cool stuff about you in the process who knows... ?*

Create and show YourSphere and get some cool stuff ! This YSWS is all about you and whatever makes you who you are and YOU ! 

## Why does this YSWS exists ??

*what are you calling a soft skill ???????*  

Well if I had to sum it up in one sentence I would say "portfolios are booooooriiiing 🎀", now if I had to dissert, I would say that I believe that we're not ourselves when we make something about us for other people.  
And don't get it wrong, I don't think that all portfolios are bad, I just think they're the worst thing you could give to someone if you wanted them to get to know you ❤  
Sorry not sorry but I believe we're too interesting to choose to present what we do that is "conventionnaly interesting" instead of who we are. I'd would prefer to know that you spend a whole summer playing Lost in blue 2 on your DS just to find that your save data has been corrupted after the cartridge has spent like 5 months in a pouch (actually the cartridge just needed to be reinserted my save is okay) than you have a degree in hoo hee hoo haa college in science of engineering.  
Again don't get it wrong, knowledge is important, but school isn't always the best place to learn, I could perhaps think of some example right now... Hack Club maybe ?  
Anywaysssss that's why I wanted to design an event that makes room for our diverse-complex-not-fitting-in-any-box identities. Who we actually are, making introspection funnily fun.  
I believe human beings have so much to learn from each other, and it only applies when we're deeply honnest with ourselves, which can happen when we building our own space, or our own *sphere*.  
So here comes MySphere™ (not an actual trade mark), a boring school project (apparently worth 11/20 but it's okay) that became a personnal website (don't check it out) that now become your and my opportunity to get to know each other by building our own pages. A sphere for you, yourself and us 😝  
Anywayssssssss now that we answered the *why* let's get into the what and how and additionnal fun terms and conditions (while writing this I can't think of any term or condition but I think it's just cool to throw this type of words so please laugh or idk be more happy, edit: condition #1 be a nice person, both nice and a person).  
Ok so in one sentence (again), the goal of this event is to allow you to choose what you think is worth showing. 
Redefine what is "interesting", burn (figuratively) the profesionnalism, stomp (again figuratively or not, you choose) on that boring resume and please I beg you try to go to an interview with the website you'll make here and tell me how it went, I bet you it will be fun.

## Important Asked Questions  

Now let's talk about this YSWS, shall we ?

### "Gurl what am I supposed to do ??"    

I'm glad you asked Fatima, you'll find in [this](not-existant-yet.com) repo the basic models you will HAVE TO use as a base for your page.  
As you may have guessed (or if you just looked at the files) these models are blank, boring and lifeless (like most of portfolios, no shade (actually yes shade)).    
You're supposed to take the files and edit them, and this involves (take notes):  
- add your own unique aesthetic/chaos in the background, with stickers, art, a song made with a Wario Ware game and recorded with a dusty microphone or just a basic wii theme (booooriiing (jk (I'm actually kidding wii theme songs are nice but if you make your own song you get some extra *points de coolitude*))),  
- fill the page with irrelevant stories, facts about you or something dear to you (but make it about you !). Just keep in mind that you are not going to present this in an interview, THIS IS NOT A PORTFOLIO.
- the model itself already looks like a social media page, a bit retro maybe, you can add some posts (design them in a way that suits the theme of your page or don't it's your sphere), share some photos like what's in your airfryer (mine has a 4+ times used baking paper (I'll throw it when it will burn, not before)), what your animal crossing new leaf room looks like (mine isn't that of a mess thankfully).
- extra stuff: easter eggs hidden in your profile photo, memes that makes you laugh (best if you make them yourself girlypop)

Now Fatima I know you didn't asked this but I would still like to tell you what you MUST NOT do/put in your page:
- photos of you/other beautiful people, we all know you're BEAUTIFUL but yeah no, it's not the gram here. Be mindful, think of who could see these, think of AI and stuff (girl it's fine if you're putting your 3DS or PC setup just keep this in mind).  
- personnal details of people in general, please don't gossip in this page we're all peace and love here. What I mean by gossip is telling someone's personnal story/infos with or without their name and without their consent. Please use your common sense™ and spread love 🌈
- personnal details about you, yes it's about you, and it's about what you want to share with the world. No one should and want to know that you painted your walls with your doo-doo when you were five, this is crazy btw and we would rather want to know what your Tomodachi island looks like. Be mindful and don't play with poop that's a bit gross (I'm not accusing anyone here). Share what's important for you or just some silly stuff, and again "Please use your common sense™ and spread love 🌈".

### How do I get my stuff ??

Short answer: with badges.  
Long answer: well this event will work pretty much like the café event tha took place a loooooooong (not so long) time ago while being different.  
You shall work on YourSphere following these step / this process:
- First, you will choose a model, start building your page, being creative, telling you stuff blah blah blah, after meeting the requirements, you'll earn your first badge (you will need some of those to unlock your prizes) and your page will be elligible to be showcased in MySphere's page ! 🎺
> The requirements: (22/03 version)
> - a profile with a bio, a profile picture, and at least one additional section (what is in it is up to you).
> - images, at least one profile picture and some (at least 4 excluding your pp) images here and there on the main part of your page. main part != background.
> - basic personalization stuff which includes and is not exclusive to fonts, colors and background image and can include animations? Oh and please I personnaly hate portfolios ready-to-use carrousels or "hover me I'll glow" buttons. For example if you want to make a carrousel make it the way you would like it, not exclusively the way you know it catches the eye. You could make it look like a book's page or a pile of polaroid photos or idk something special !
> - some text, and by "some" I mean this is YourSphere, put as much text as you but not 98.8% but as much as you want (make it cool for you to read and be yourself !)
> - some posts, i.e. text and an/multiple image(s) in a post looking design (that you choose and design), at least 4 of those !
> - at least one unique thing you created that is not mentionned here, because you will create basically everything of your page but we (me and I) want you to do something pretty much unprecedented and unexpected and put it in your page. For the n-th time, be creative !  
Once you're "done" (for this first step at least), it going to be more technical to earn/unlock more badges. Everytime you will complete one of the tasks shared on the MySphere Member Dashboard you will earn a certain amount of badges, the MSMD will be shared in the channel [#mysphere](https://hackclub.enterprise.slack.com/archives/C0AN449805A) created for this event so stay tuned 💥  
These badges are not a currency ! Once they're earned, they're earned.  
Now when I said that "this event will work pretty much like the café event while being different", I'm going to make it make sense.

### How do I actually get my stuff ??

When you will manage to earn badges you will first get a .png file with the badge you earned so you can put it on YourSphere AND more exciting part you will get this exact badge shipped to your front door (or a Mondial Relay locker idk where you live).  
BUT I'm not done talking, you will earn prizes according to the number of bagdes you earned in total.
Now there are some prizes you can earn by stacking your badges and there are some prizes that require a certain badge or set of badges, you will see, again stay tuned ‼
