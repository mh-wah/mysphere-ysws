Build YourSphere... but what is YourSphere ??

YourSphere is basically the page where you exist on the wonderful platform that is MySphere (don't take it too literally it's just a ysws but if your work is tea you might be showcased).

You will basically have to use a model of MySphere pages and start building your own unique page around it ! Make it as personnal and unprofessionnal as you want because I'm so sick and tired (and I know that deep down you too) of serious portfolios made of 80% of uiverse.io css code. We want to get to know who you actually are (and not necessarly what you do) and this YSWS is the occasion to show it to the world (so do it) !

Build a page, experiment, add some myspherical features and have fun yapping about how interesting you are because you are ! Each and everyone of us can be so interesting, even what looks insignificant could be the beginning of a friendship !
Your page could be looking like a collage of Vogue magazines, a snap of what a Y2Kified happy new year looks like for you, your hidden poetry corner or just a 2075's fall album for you !

Just be you, that's the most original thing YourSphere could be about.

... and get some personnal brand stuff ! (that's what we ship)

And by personnal brand stuff I mean actual stuff to show people who you are +other useful stuff (and even if it weren't useful I'm sure you would take it cuz it's free and I know I would take it).

And personnal brand stuff is the most appropriate expression I would use since if I had put what I said in the last paragraph in the title my h2 tag would explode (yes it happens sometimes).

This includes and is certainly not limiled to your own NFC not-official-yet Hack Club ID which would eventually become official if we conquer the world (which I don't think anyone is planning don't put me in jail), grants for pins and badges for what you stand for, microsd cards if you're the kind of aesthetic individual that loves mini cameras or just any thing with a sd port and as often if not always, sticker sheets and stickers (yay !) and why not plushies if you break the ceiling with your badges or you just stand out with a unanimously (such a complicated word...) lovely design so you can be You © with someone else.

I. building YourSphere

Use one of the models and build your own page about you. You'll have to create a page in which you tell the world who you are and what you want them to know about you (cuz who wants to read a boring resume nowadays...) see the requirements of your first page

II. earn your first badge

Now that the first version of YourSphere is looking all good and unprofessionnal, you can be proud of yourself because you just earned your first badge !

Bagdes will allow you to earn some awesomely cool prizes, gotta catch 'em all ! (I don't even like pokemon that much but anyways) Badges are not a currency, think of them as steps to a milestone (or cup for one who attended the café event, we see you).

III. keep earning badges I guess

Did you think it would be over ?? Sorry to disapoint you. Actually I'm not disapointing anyone here because more stuff to do == more opportunities to get stuff so rejoice, the fun is not over ! In order to win more badges you will have to add some of the myspherical features to your masterpiece.

You will find the myspherical features you can code and adapt to the unique style of your page here and the list of stuff you will earn according to the number of bagdes you have will be revealed soon so stay tuned in the #mysphere channel on Slack !
